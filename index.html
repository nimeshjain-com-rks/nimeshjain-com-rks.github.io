Founder's Message

In the history of Bhagwan Mahaveer Shiksha & Vikas Samiti, Rishabhdeo, the academic session 2011-12 proved to be an important milestone. It is in this session that one of its units, Mahaveer Public School, got its 10+2 C.B.S.E affiliation. Today, I am pleased to learn that the first batch of its Sr. Sec. students is appearing for the board Exams2014-15. We hope and pray That the students come out in flying colors. The other unit, i.e., MVM had started the Sr. Sec. Classes with Science, Commerce and Arts faculties in the year 2010. We hope for the best results in these classes this year as well. In our Dungarpur unit (Vardaan International School) we are starting class x from the coming session 2015-2016 Now it has a strength of around 1000 students. The plans are on the anvil to affiliate it to the CBSE in the near future. We are getting good feedback about this school from the parents and public of the city, We have added one more feather to our cap by adding another unit in Chawand named Neo Valley International School. The unit includes a school up to class XII 1200 students and a Girls’College with 200 students. We are happy that all our schools are doing very well in imparting quality education to young minds of the areas. The credit of the success goes to the Management members, Head of the schools, the teachers and others who are supporting it from behind the curtain. I thank them all from the bottom of my heart. I also congratulate the Sanchalika’ of the SAMITI Mrs. Leelawati Kikawat for her efforts in establishing the SAMITI and the success she has gained in a short time. I thank all the parents and the well-wishers of the school whose cooperation this institute could not have taken this shape. Thanking you all once again. Mr. S.L. Jain - Founder - Director
